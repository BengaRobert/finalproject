Courses We Offer

«Ultimate Ski» offers a wide range of individual and group lessons, where you can choose a course according to your requirements.
Take the fast track in improving your skills on the mountain and have the ultimate learning experience.
Book Your Lessons Today!

Beginner

If you don’t have previous experience in alpine skiing, don't worry! We teach you the basics and you get to know the equipment. We practice how to fall down safely and getting up independently, sliding, turning and how to stop. Training will be held on flat and easy parts of the slope. You become familiar with ski lift and slope safety.

Intermediate

You can ski easy slopes with snow plough turns and stop in a controlled manner. We develop confidence and speed in your skiing. The aim is to learn to parallel ski with controlled manner, to use different types of ski lifts and taking steps to more demanding slopes.

Pro

You can ski all types of slopes in a controllable manner and with good speed. We train different turning techniques on wider variety of slopes and snow conditions. According to the participant’ preferences, we can learn different slope tricks and get a taste of freestyle skiing in park. The aim is to become a versatile skier and to expand your skiing skills.
